# Kraft Heinz (kraft-heinz)
The Kraft Heinz Company is one of the largest food and beverage companies in the world, formed by the merger of Kraft Foods and H.J. Heinz Company. The company manufactures and markets food and beverage products including condiments, sauces, cheese, dairy, meals, meats, refreshment beverages, coffee, and other grocery products. Kraft Heinz does not currently offer a publicly documented developer API.
